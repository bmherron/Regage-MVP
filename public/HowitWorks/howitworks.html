Crowdfunding with Regage

Regage is the easiest way to get funding for your project. Regage shares your project with your peers and ‘hometown’ network as soon as you finish creating it. Use Regage and your large network to crowdfund your next idea or event!

Pledging

Regage uses an All-or-Nothing funding model so, as a backer, you are not charged unless the project has raised the amount it needs to be successful. Simply enter the amount you wish to pledge and your card information with Stripe, our easy and secure payment processor. **Will revise for launch to allow for hybrid funding models** **Also can remove the Strip stuff bc it will be below, maybe only focus on the secure and quick process.**

Fees

We charge 8% and $0.30 per transaction. 2.9% and $0.30 of that fee goes to our payment processor, Stripe. Are you a registered non-profit? Look for our special rates below! **Not happy with but I am moving on**

Non-profits

Regage is here to help you! Pay NO Regage fees by sending us your 501©3(?) when you create your project. Only pay fees to Stripe for payment processing!

Helping Non-Profits

Regage is committed to doing its part in the community. We want Non-profits to worry about doing social good and less about how to pay the bills. That is why we offer our platform free of charge to all registered Non-Profits!

Private User Data

Regage does not sell your private data to third parties. Unlike other social networks, we have no need to sell your data or monetize it. We make money from crowdfunding projects, that is it.

No Advertisements

Regage does not attempt to monetize from your data. As a result of that promise, you will not see ads from third party advertisers so can be assured your data is safe with us. **Not sold on all of this**
